ABOUT US

We are the Gyru App company based in Kent, United Kingdom. We started our journey with the idea of making an App that will connect people, who want to learn driving, with the best driving instructors. Our motto is to help people find their instructors through the app and make their life easier.

Concepts

Our main concept is to connect the best driving instructors with the learners via a simple App. In any location where Gyru app operates, a user can easily find a driving instructor and start learning to drive. Moreover, experts affiliated with App industry have worked with the concept.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect the learner with the best driving instructor in the shortest possible time without any hassle. Also, we want to help users avoid unwanted gap during a driving lesson and eradicate the difficulty of making an appointment to learn driving.

Our Obstacles

Wireframe sketch is neat, but some dots were not connecting to each other. One of our experts came up with some brilliant yet simple solution to connect the dots.

It was difficult for us to choose the right platform for back-end functionalities. Finally, we decided to work on Firebase.

We are lacking professional instructors on board. In this circumstance, our company director appointed an approved driving instructor (ADI).

Our Promise

Our promise is to make the process as easy and simple as possible. We are making this app in a way that will benefit both the learners and instructors.

Conclusion

In this era of fast-moving world, driving is an unavoidable need/In this fast-moving-era, driving is an essential need. As a result, people look for the best instructor to learn driving and we help them connect. We would like to be remembered by turning their problem into solution.
